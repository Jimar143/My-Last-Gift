Ikaw at Ako

TJ Monterde • Memories of Us
Happy birthday, my greatest love ^^ Sorry kung ‘di ko na naman natupad yung pangako ko sa’yo 🥺 Gusto ko sana ibigay sa’yo personally yung ilang months kong ginawa na charcoal portraits, pero hindi kami nanalo 😞 Kaya gumawa na lang ako ng lifetime website na para sa’yo lang ^^ Ikaw lang makaka-access basta ‘di mo mawawala yung link at ‘di mo ipapa open sa iba 😄 Sana magustuhan mo ^^. It’s been 3 or 4 years na rin pala since wala na tayo… Every time I hear "Ikaw at Ako" by TJ Monterde, I can't help but think of you. It brings back so many memories, our laughter, those long conversation that felt like they could last forever. The song reminds me of how special you were to me and the moments we shared. Masaya ako na nakilala kita—not just as a partner, but as someone who brought so much joy and inspiration into my life. Eto na siguro yung pinaka huling regalo ko sa’yo, love ^^ Gusto ko sana magpaalam sa’yo in person for the last time bago ako pumunta sa Canada… pero kahit binigay ko na best ko, hindi pa rin kami nanalo😔. Ang dami ko sanang gustong sabihin sa’yo sa personal….pero mukhang hanggang dito na lang talaga😔 Nung time na may nakilala kang iba, pinili kong ilayo muna yung sarili ko para hindi ako maging abala sainyo 😓 that time Halo-halo yung naramdaman ko—nalungkot, kasi yung taong pinapasaya ko dati, may iba nang nagpapasaya😄… pero at the same time, masaya rin ako, kasi alam kong may taong nandyan para ibigay sa’yo yung mga bagay na gusto ko sanang maibigay, pero hindi ko na nagawa^^ 1y Mahigit din kayo, noh? Dun pa lang alam kong mas better siya sakin HAHAHA. Kaya tinanggap ko na lang din that time na masaya kana ^^ Sana matupad mo lahat ng pangarap mo^^. Siguro hanggang dito na lang talaga yung kwento nating dalawa. Susuportahan pa rin naman kita from afar, pero baka hindi na ako magpaparamdam. Sorry for not keeping my promise to you again 😞, and I’m really sorry for avoiding you. Sorry for everything I’ve done that hurt you or made things harder between us 🥺. Hindi man ako yung pinaka-best na taong nakilala mo, but I hope one day when you hear my name, mapapangiti ka na lang and say, “I miss this person.” ^^ I hope that if we meet again someday… I hope we’re both already happy ^^. Happy birthday ulit, Pra Pra ^^… Alam kong ilang beses ko nang sinabi sa’yo noon na “I love you for the last time", pero siguro eto na talaga yun. I love you, love ^^ Ingatan mo palagi sarili mo 🥺
